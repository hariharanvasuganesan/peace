About Us

"Prevention is better than cure." This is works for mental health more than anything else right now.

The onset of the pandemic led to worldwide lockdowns with people stuck at home and in their heads. Not being able to see loved ones in person, not being able to go out socially, has affected everyone. And that is why Peace NGO is focussed to provide therapy consultations to people from all walks of life in Chennai. We have partnered with top mental health specialists in the city who have volunteered to provide FREE therapy for those who are unable to afford them.

Use our 'Find a Therapist' feature to register to our website and book an appointment with a specialist. Any specialists willing to provide their time and volunteer with us are requested to at the volunteer page. People looking to provide monetary support are asked to find the necessary details at the 'DONATE NOW' button.
